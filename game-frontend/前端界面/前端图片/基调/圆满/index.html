Spring Summer Autumn Winter
The story finds its light
🌸
Spring
First Light
Through thaw and tender green, the promise under the soil holds:
no winter is the last word—only the opening
of what was always meant to blossom into a fulfilled end.
☀️
Summer
High Noon
In the long gold of day we learn that joy need not fear its closing;
every warmth we kindle here lights the way
to the homecoming written in the heart of the story.
🍂
Autumn
Harvest
What the wind takes from the branch it sets in the open hand as gift;
we gather what we were given and walk, lightened,
into the final chapter we always deserved.
❄️
Winter
Stillness
Snow writes the hush we need to hear the faithful pulse beneath;
in the coldest breath the circle completes—
and the ending arrives, whole, gentle, and fulfilled.
